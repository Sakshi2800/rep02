10 Fascinating Facts About Crocodiles

  • Crocodiles have a unique tooth replacement system that allows them to grow and replace thousands of teeth over their lifetime.
  • They can live for several decades, with some individuals reaching over 70 years of age.
  • Saltwater crocodiles are the largest living reptiles in the world, with adults averaging 3 to 5 meters in length.
  • Crocodiles have excellent senses, including good night vision and a great sense of smell.
  • Nile crocodiles are ambush predators, waiting for hours or days for the right moment to attack.
  • Crocodiles open their mouths to help cool themselves down, a process called gaping.
  • Glands under their chin release a special scent which they use to recognize each other.
  • Crocodiles are cold-blooded, meaning they cannot produce their own heat.
  • A crocodile's bite is stronger and more powerful than a great white shark.
  • Crocodiles eat fish, frogs, birds, reptiles, and even large animals like hippos and buffalo.
